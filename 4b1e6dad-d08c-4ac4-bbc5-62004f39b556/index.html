How to get a Backlink on cosap.org
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Create user"
Step 3: Click on "Submit a New Story"
Step 4: Enter your website URL and click on "Continue"
Step 5: Fill in with short description, enter tags, choose a category, then click on "Save Changes and Submit"
Step 6: Click on "Submit Story"
Step 7: You will find your link here
