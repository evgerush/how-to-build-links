How to get a Backlink on hub.docker.com
Step 1: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign Up"
Step 2: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 3: Sign in with your credentials
Step 4: Click on "Repositories"
Step 5: Click on "Create Repository"
Step 6: Create a title (no spaces, no captions, single word), enter your website URL, set visibility to "Public", then click on "Create"
Step 7: Click on the pencil icon next to the "Readme" section
Step 8: Fill in with "<a href="https://YOURWEBSITE.com">WEBSITE NAME</a>" (replace with your website details), then click on "Update"
Step 9: You will find your link here
