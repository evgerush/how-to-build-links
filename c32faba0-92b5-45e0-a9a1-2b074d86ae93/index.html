How to get a Backlink on wonderhowto.com
Step 1: Click on "Login or Signup"
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Join"
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 4: Create a password and username, set your birthday, then click on "Next"
Step 5: You can fill in with some extra information or skip this step
Step 6: Upload your profile picture
Step 7: Select "Settings"
Step 8: Enter your website URL and click on "Update Profile Settings"
Step 9: You will find your link here
