How to get a Backlink on myopportunity.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Select one of the opportunities, then click on "Next"
Step 3: Fill in the required fields and click on "Next"
Step 4: Feel free to add your interests, then click on "Next"
Step 5: Select industries you work with, then click on "Next"
Step 6: Click on "Next" - you can add your linkedin profile link
Step 7: Select gender and age of your desired connections, then click on "Next"
Step 8: Select your location, then click on "Next"
Step 9: Add your profile picture and click on "Create account"
Step 10: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 11: Select "Networker" in the top right corner
Step 12: Enter your website URL
Step 13: Click on "Save"
Step 14: You will find your link here
