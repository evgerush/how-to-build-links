How to get a Backlink on flickr.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign up"
Step 3: Check your mailbox for email from Flickr
Step 4: Confirm your account by clicking on the provided link
Step 5: Once verified, click on "Okay, got It!"
Step 6: Sign in with your credentials
Step 7: Click on the profile picture, then select "Settings"
Step 8: Click on "Change" next to your real name
Step 9: Enter your website details into the Online bits section
Step 10: Click on "Save It"
Step 11: You will find your link here
