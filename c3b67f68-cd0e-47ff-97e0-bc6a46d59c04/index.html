How to get a Backlink on linksplit.io
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Create Account"
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 4: Click on "Create a Short URL"
Step 5: Enter your website URL and click on "Shorten URL"
Step 6: You will find your link here
