How to get a Backlink on zoho.com
Step 1: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign Up"
Step 2: Choose a template
Step 3: Create a site name, then click on "Create Site"
Step 4: Fill in with title, short description and your website URL, then click on "Publish"
Step 5: Create your subdomain (free) and click on "Proceed to Publish"
Step 6: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 7: Click on "Verify"
Step 8: You will find your link here
