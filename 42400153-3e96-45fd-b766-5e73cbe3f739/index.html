How to get a Backlink on deviantart.com
Step 1: Click on "Join" to create your account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Join"
Step 3: Choose "Promote Myself" and click on "Continue"
Step 4: Pick at least 3 topics you like and click on "Continue"
Step 5: Click on the link from confirmation email in your mailbox
Step 6: Log in to your account
Step 7: Click on "Edit"
Step 8: Fill in with your website URL and Name in the "About" tab
Step 9: Then put your website URL in the "BIO" tab and click on "Save"
Step 10: You will find your links here ( number 1 - No-Follow link; number 2 - Do-Follow link)
