How to get a Backlink on exposure.co
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Create Account"
Step 3: Click on "Start Storytelling"
Step 4: Click on "Start First Story"
Step 5: Upload picture - this will be your story cover photo
Step 6: Fill in with title, short description and your website URL, then click on "Publish Story"
Step 7: Click on "Publish Story Now"
Step 8: You will find your link here
