How to get a Backlink on plurk.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign up at Plurk!"
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 4: Enter your website URL and click on "Plurk"
Step 5: You will find your link here
