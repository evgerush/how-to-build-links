How to get a Backlink on vecteezy.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign Up Free"
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 4: Select "Settings" in the top right corner
Step 5: Enter your website URL and click on "Save Changes"
Step 6: You will find your link here
