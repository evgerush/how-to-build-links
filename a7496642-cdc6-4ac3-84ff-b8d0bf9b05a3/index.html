How to get a Backlink on tuugo.co.cr
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Select "Post my Company, Products and Services"
Step 3: Fill in the required fields and click on "Register"
Step 4: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 5: Fill in the required fields and click on "Add my Business now" (don't forget to enter your website URL)
Step 6: Use a map to set your business location
Step 7: You will find your link here
