How to get a Backlink on write.as
Step 1: Click on "Create a Blog"
Step 2: Click on "Start for Free"
Step 3: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign Up"
Step 4: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 5: Click on "Write"
Step 6: Fill in with title, short description and your website URL, then click on "Publish" (Use "link" button to add link)
Step 7: You will find your link here
