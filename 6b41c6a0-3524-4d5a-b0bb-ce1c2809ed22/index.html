How to get a Backlink on crevado.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Choose a free plan
Step 3: Fill in the required fields and click on "Create My Online Portfolio"
Step 4: Click on "Continue"
Step 5: Click on "Upload" and choose your PDF File that contains your website URL
Step 6: Fill in with title, short description and your website URL, then click on "Save" (Use "link" button to add link)
Step 7: You will find your link here
