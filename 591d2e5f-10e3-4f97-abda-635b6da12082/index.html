How to get a Backlink on tupalo.co
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Enter your email address and click on "Join Tupalo"
Step 3: You will find your password in your mailbox
Step 4: Select "Settings" in the top right corner
Step 5: Enter your website URL
Step 6: Click on "Save"
Step 7: You will find your link here
