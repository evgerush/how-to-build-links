How to get a Backlink on digitalcashpalace.com
Step 1: Go to https://digitalcashpalace.com/forum/ and open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Register"
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on confirmation link
Step 4: Click on "Edit your account details"
Step 5: Enter your website URL, then click on "Save"
Step 6: You will find your link here
