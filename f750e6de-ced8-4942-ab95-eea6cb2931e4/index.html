How to get a Backlink on dribbble.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Create Account"
Step 3: Choose an avatar, fill in with your location, then click on "Next"
Step 4: Select "I'm looking for design inspiration", then click on "Finish"
Step 5: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 6: Click on "Edit Profile"
Step 7: Enter your website URL and click on "Save Profile"
Step 8: You will find your link here
