How to get a Backlink on bravenet.com
Step 1: Go to https://www.bravenet.com/sitebuilder and click on "Build Your Site Now"
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Create Account"
Step 3: Enter verification code that you will find in your mailbox and click on "Continue"
Step 4: Choose a theme and click on "Create Site"
Step 5: Choose a Bravenet free subdomain, create your website address, then click on "Create Site"
Step 6: Click on "Start Building Website"
Step 7: Fill in with title, short description and your website URL - all changes will be saved automatically
Step 8: You will find your link here
