How to get a Backlink on wanelo.co
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Click on "Join us!"
Step 3: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign up"
Step 4: Click on "Skip this step"
Step 5: Select one from the options and click on "Next"
Step 6: Select "Settings" in the top right corner
Step 7: Enter your website URL
Step 8: Click on "Save Profile"
Step 9: You will find your link here
