How to get a Backlink on forum.unity.com
Step 1: Click on "Create a Unity ID"
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Create a Unity ID"
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 4: Click on "Verify"
Step 5: Sign in with your credentials
Step 6: Go to "https://forum.unity.com/" and choose "General Discussion"
Step 7: Click on "Post New Thread"
Step 8: Fill in with title, description and your website URL, add tags, then click on "Create Thread" (Use "link" button to add link)
Step 9: You will find your link here
