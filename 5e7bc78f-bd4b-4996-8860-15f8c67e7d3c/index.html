How to get a Backlink on kutt.it
Step 1: Click on "Log in/Sign up"
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign Up"
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 4: Click on "Back to homepage"
Step 5: Enter your website URL and click on the designated button
Step 6: You will find your link here
