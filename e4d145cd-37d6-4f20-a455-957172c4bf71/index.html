How to get a Backlink on jovoto.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Select to "Sign Up with Email"
Step 3: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign Up"
Step 4: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 5: Fill in the required fields and click on "Submit"
Step 6: Click on "Go to Newsfeed"
Step 7: Select "Settings" in the top right corner
Step 8: Click on "Creative background"
Step 9: Enter your website URL
Step 10: Click on "Save Changes"
Step 11: You will find your link here
