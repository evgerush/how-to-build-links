How to get a Backlink on calameo.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Create My Free Account"
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 4: Select "Individual"
Step 5: Click on "Go to my Dashboard"
Step 6: Select "Settings"
Step 7: Click on "Account info"
Step 8: Enter your website URL and click on "Update Info"
Step 9: You will find your link here
