How to get a Backlink on qr.net
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Enter your email address and click on "Sign Up"
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link (You will also find your password here)
Step 4: Select "Rediretc to a website", enter your website URL and click on "Create QR Code"
Step 5: You will find your link here
