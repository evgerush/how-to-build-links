How to get a Backlink on 500px.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Click on "Continue with email"
Step 3: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign up"
Step 4: Fill in with your name, then click on "Next" (Username will be generated automatically based on your email address but you can change it)
Step 5: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 6: Select "Profile" in the top right corner
Step 7: Click on the pencil icon
Step 8: Enter your website URL and click on "Save changes"
Step 9: You will find your link here
