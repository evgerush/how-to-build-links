How to get a Backlink on all4webs.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and solve the puzzle to continue
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 4: Fill in with title, headline, short description and your website URL, then click on "Save Settings!" (Use "link" button to insert link)
Step 5: Click on the provided link
Step 6: You will find your link here
