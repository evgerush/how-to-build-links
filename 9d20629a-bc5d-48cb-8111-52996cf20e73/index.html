How to get a Backlink on qualtrics.com
Step 1: Click on "Login"
Step 2: Choose to "Sign Up"
Step 3: Fill in the required fields and click on "Create Account"
Step 4: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 5: Fill in the required fields and click on "Continue"
Step 6: Click on "Next"
Step 7: Click on "Continue"
Step 8: Click on "Get started"
Step 9: Name your project, then click on "Create project"
Step 10: Create question that contains your website URL and click on "Publish"
Step 11: Click on "Publish"
Step 12: Copy the provided link and open it in your browser
Step 13: You will find your link here
