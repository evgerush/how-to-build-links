How to get a Backlink on uid.me
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign up"
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 4: Rewrite the security code, then click on "Send"
Step 5: Click on "Personal details"
Step 6: Go to "Websites" tab, enter your website URL and click on "Add"
Step 7: You will find your link here
