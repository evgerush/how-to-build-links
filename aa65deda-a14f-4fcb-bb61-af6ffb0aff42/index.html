How to get a Backlink on foroactivo.com
Step 1: The website is in foreign language with no option to choose language, that’s why we suggest using Chrome browser and translating the page to English
Step 2: Enter the desired forum name and click on "Create a forum"
Step 3: Fill in the required fields and click on the designated button
Step 4: Confirm your password and click on "Discover My Forum"
Step 5: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 6: Proceed to your forum
Step 7: Click on "Your first forum"
Step 8: Select "Post new topic"
Step 9: Fill in with title, short description and your website URL, then click on "Submit" (Use "link" button to add link)
Step 10: You will find your link here
