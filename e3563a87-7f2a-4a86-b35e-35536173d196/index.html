How to get a Backlink on www.vox.com
Step 1: Go to "https://www.vox.com/", scroll to the bottom of the page and click on "Author Login"
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign Up"
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 4: Go to "https://www.vox.com/", scroll to the bottom of the page and click on "Edit Profile"
Step 5: Fill in with short description and your website URL, then click on "Update"
Step 6: You will find your link here
