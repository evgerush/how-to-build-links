How to get a Backlink on imgur.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Next"
Step 3: Enter 7-digit verification code that you should recieve via SMS and click on "Register"
Step 4: Click on "About"
Step 5: Click on the pencil icon
Step 6: Enter your website URL and click on "Save"
Step 7: You will find your link here
