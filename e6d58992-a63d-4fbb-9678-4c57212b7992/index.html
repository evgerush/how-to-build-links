How to get a Backlink on feedsfloor.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Free Sign Up"
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 4: Click on "Edit"
Step 5: Once more, fill in with your "Company Name" and "Website"
Step 6: Fill in with a short description
Step 7: Select "Type of organization" and click on "Save"
Step 8: You will find your link here
