How to get a Backlink on myanimelist.net
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Create Account"
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 4: Select "Profile" in the top right corner
Step 5: Click on "Write it now"
Step 6: Enter your website URL and click on "Submit"
Step 7: You will find your link here
