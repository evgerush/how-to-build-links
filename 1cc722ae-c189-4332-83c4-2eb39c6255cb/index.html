How to get a Backlink on github.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields
Step 3: Verify your account by solving the captcha (1) and clicko on "Create account" (2)
Step 4: Click on "Your profile"
Step 5: Select "Edit profile"
Step 6: Fill in with your website URL
Step 7: Click on "Update profile"
Step 8: You will find your link here
