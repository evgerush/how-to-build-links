How to get a Backlink on gumroad.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Create account"
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 4: Fill in the required fields, enter a short introduction and your website URL into the "Bio" section, then click on "Next" Add your first product"
Step 5: Go to "https://gumroad.com/YOURUSERNAME", you will find your link here
