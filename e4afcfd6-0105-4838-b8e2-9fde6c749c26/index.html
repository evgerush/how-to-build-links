How to get a Backlink on patreon.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign up"
Step 3: Click on "Verify Email"
Step 4: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 5: Click on "My profile" in the top right corner
Step 6: Click on "Edit Profile"
Step 7: Choose your Country of Residence
Step 8: Fill in "About you" section with short introduction and your website URL
Step 9: Click on "Save changes"
Step 10: You will find your link here
