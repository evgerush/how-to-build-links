How to get a Backlink on w3techs.com
Step 1: Click on "Users"
Step 2: Choose to "Register"
Step 3: Fill in the required fields and click on "Submit"
Step 4: Click on "Websites"
Step 5: Enter your website URL and click on "Submit"
Step 6: You will find your link here
