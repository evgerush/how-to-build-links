How to get a Backlink on everforo.com
Step 1: Click on "Get Started For Free"
Step 2: Enter your email address and click on "Continue"
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 4: Create your username, choose your profile picture and create a password, then click on "Continue"
Step 5: Click on "Get Started For Free"
Step 6: Choose cover photos, name your group and add a description, then click on "Create"
Step 7: Click on "Post a new topic"
Step 8: Fill in with title, short description and your website URL, then click on "Send" (Use "link" button to add link)
Step 9: You will find your link here
