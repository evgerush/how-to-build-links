How to get a Backlink on bc.vc
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields, then click on "Register"
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on confirmation link
Step 4: Go to "Login Page"
Step 5: Login with your credentials
Step 6: Enter your website URL, then click on "Shorten"
Step 7: You will find your link here
