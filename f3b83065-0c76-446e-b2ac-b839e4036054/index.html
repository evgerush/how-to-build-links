How to get a Backlink on pubhtml5.com
Step 1: Click on "Create Your Publication"
Step 2: Click on "Upload Online"
Step 3: Click on "Sign up for a free account"
Step 4: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign Up"
Step 5: Enter verification code that you will find in your mailbox and click on "Verify Account"
Step 6: Click on "Default" folder
Step 7: Select "Add New Book"
Step 8: Upload your PDF file that contains your website URL, then click on "Next"
Step 9: Fill in with title and short description, add keywords, choose category, then click on "Create"
Step 10: You will find your link here
