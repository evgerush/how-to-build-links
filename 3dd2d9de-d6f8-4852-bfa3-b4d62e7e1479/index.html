How to get a Backlink on wordpress.org
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Create Account"
Step 3: Check your mailbox for email with verification link and click on it
Step 4: Create a password and click on "Create Account"
Step 5: Click on "Edit Profile" in the top right corner
Step 6: Click on "Edit Profile"
Step 7: Fill in with your name - it can be username
Step 8: Enter your website URL
Step 9: Click on "Save Changes"
Step 10: You will find your link here
