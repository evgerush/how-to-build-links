How to get a Backlink on addwish.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in with your email address and name, then click on "Sign Up"
Step 3: Create a name for your list
Step 4: Click on "View List"
Step 5: Click on "New"
Step 6: Enter your website URL and click on "Add"
Step 7: Click on "Save"
Step 8: You will find your link here
