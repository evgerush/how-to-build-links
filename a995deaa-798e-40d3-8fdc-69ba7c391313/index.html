How to get a Backlink on wattpad.com
Step 1: Open an account by clicking on "Start Writing"
Step 2: Click on "Sign up with Email"
Step 3: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign up with email"
Step 4: Select "I like to write", then click on "Next"
Step 5: Click on "Skip"
Step 6: Give some more information and click on "Next"
Step 7: Select at least 3 genres, then click on "Finish"
Step 8: Click on "Start reading"
Step 9: Select "Settings" in the top right corner
Step 10: Enter your website URL
Step 11: Click on "Save"
Step 12: You will find your link here
