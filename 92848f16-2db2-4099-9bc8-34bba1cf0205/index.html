How to get a Backlink on travellerspoint.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Join Travellerspoint"
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 4: Click on "Write a Blog Entry"
Step 5: Create a title and subtitle for your blog, then click on "Create the Blog"
Step 6: Click on "Add your first entry"
Step 7: Fill in with title, short description and your website URL, then click on "Publish" (Use "link" button to add link)
Step 8: You will find your link here
