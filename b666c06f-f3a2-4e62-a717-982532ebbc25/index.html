How to get a Backlink on xtgem.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign Up"
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 4: Choose template for your website
Step 5: Select color preset, then click on "Next"
Step 6: Click on "Next"
Step 7: Fill in with title, short description and your website URL, then click on "Confirm" (Use "link" button to add link)
Step 8: Click on the provided link
Step 9: You will find your link here
