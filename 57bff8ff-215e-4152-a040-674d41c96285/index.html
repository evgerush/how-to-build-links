How to get a Backlink on polygon.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign Up"
Step 3: check the reCAPTCHA and click on "Sign Up"
Step 4: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 5: Select "Edit Profile" in the top right corner
Step 6: Fill in with your website URL and name, then click on "Update"
Step 7: You will find your link here
