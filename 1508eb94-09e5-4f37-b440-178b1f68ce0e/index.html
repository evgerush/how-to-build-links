How to get a Backlink on bitly.com
Step 1: Enter your website URL and click on "Shorten"
Step 2: Click on "Get Started"
Step 3: Choose to sign up for free
Step 4: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign up with Email"
Step 5: Click on "Skip this step"
Step 6: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 7: Click on "Create"
Step 8: Enter your website URL and click on "Create"
Step 9: You will find your link here
