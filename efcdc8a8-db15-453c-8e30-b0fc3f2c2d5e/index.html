How to get a Backlink on turnkeylinux.org
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Create New Account"
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 4: Click on "Log In
Step 5: Create a password
Step 6: Click on "Save"
Step 7: Select "Personal details" tab
Step 8: Enter your website URL and click on "Save"
Step 9: You will find your link here
