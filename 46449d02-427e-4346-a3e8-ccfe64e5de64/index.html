How to get a Backlink on imdb.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Click on "Create a New Account"
Step 3: Fill in the required fields and click on "Create your IMDb account"
Step 4: Enter one time password that you will find in your mailbox and click on "Create your IMDb account"
Step 5: Select "Account settings" in the top right corner
Step 6: Click on "Edit profile"
Step 7: Fill in "Bio" section with a short introduction and your website URL, then click on "Save Description"
Step 8: You will find your link here
