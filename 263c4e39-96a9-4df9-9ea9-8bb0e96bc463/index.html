How to get a Backlink on fliphtml5.com
Step 1: Click on "Make a Flipping Book"
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign Up"
Step 3: Fill in with verification code from your mailbox and click on "Verify Account"
Step 4: Click on "Upload PDF" (Uplad PDF file that contains your website URL)
Step 5: Enter title, fill in the "Descrioption" field with a short description, then click on "Apply"
Step 6: You will find your link here
