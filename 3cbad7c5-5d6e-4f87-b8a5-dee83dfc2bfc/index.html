How to get a Backlink on fanpop.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign In"
Step 3: Select "Profile"
Step 4: Click on "Wall"
Step 5: Fill in with short description and your website URL, then click on "Post" (Use "link" button to add link)
Step 6: You will find your link here
