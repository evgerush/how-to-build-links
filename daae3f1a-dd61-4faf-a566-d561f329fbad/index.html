How to get a Backlink on blogfree.net
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Register"
Step 3: Click on the gear icon
Step 4: Enter your website URL without "http" or "https", then click on "Save"
Step 5: You will find your link here
