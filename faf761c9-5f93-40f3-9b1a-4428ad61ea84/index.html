How to get a Backlink on tapatalk.com
Step 1: Click on "Log In"
Step 2: Open an account
Step 3: Click on "Continue With Email"
Step 4: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign Up With Email"
Step 5: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 6: Click on "Start New"
Step 7: Create a new group by filling in the required fields
Step 8: Click on "Continue"
Step 9: Click on your group
Step 10: Click on the default introduction post
Step 11: Click on "Edit"
Step 12: Enter your website URL (use the link icon)
Step 13: Click on "Submit"
Step 14: You will find your link here
