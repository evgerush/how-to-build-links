How to get a Backlink on flattr.com
Step 1: Go to https://flattr.com/creators and click on "Sign up as a creator"
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign Up"
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 4: Click on "Skip to next step"
Step 5: In the "Profile bio" section enter your website data using "[website name](website URL)" format, then click on "Save profile information"
Step 6: Click on "Skip to next step"
Step 7: Click on "Continue t dashboard"
Step 8: Select "Your profile" in the top right corner
Step 9: You will find your link here
