How to get a Backlink on hostsearch.com
Step 1: Go to http://forums.hostsearch.com and click on "Register"
Step 2: Fill in the required fields
Step 3: Click on "Complete Registration"
Step 4: Click on "Profile"
Step 5: Enter your website URL
Step 6: Click on "Save Changes"
Step 7: You will find your link here
