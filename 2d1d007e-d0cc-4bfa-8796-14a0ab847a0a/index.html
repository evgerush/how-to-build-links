How to get a Backlink on tiny.cc
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields, then click on "Start Shortening Links"
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on confirmation link
Step 4: Go to the login page
Step 5: Sign in with your credentials
Step 6: Click on "Shorten URLs"
Step 7: Enter your website URL, then click on "Shorten it!"
Step 8: You will find your link here
