How to get a Backlink on bandcamp.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Choose to "Sign up as an artist"
Step 3: Click on "Sign up"
Step 4: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign up"
Step 5: Set your location and click on "Next"
Step 6: Click on "Done"
Step 7: Click on "Add an album"
Step 8: Fill in with title, cover photo, short description and your website URL. then set on "Public" and click on "Publish"
Step 9: You will find your link here
