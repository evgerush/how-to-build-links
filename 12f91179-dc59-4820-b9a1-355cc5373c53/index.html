How to get a Backlink on ronpaulforums.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Complete Registration"
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 4: Click on "Profile"
Step 5: Enter your website URL
Step 6: Click on "Save Changes"
Step 7: You will find your link here
