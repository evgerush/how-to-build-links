How to get a Backlink on eznow.com
Step 1: Click on "Sign In"
Step 2: Open an account
Step 3: Fill in the required fields and click on "Go"
Step 4: Login with your credentials
Step 5: Click on "Website"
Step 6: Choose free option
Step 7: Create your free domain, then click on "Next"
Step 8: Choose a template
Step 9: Click on "Website Edit"
Step 10: Fill in with short description and your website URL, then click on "Save"
Step 11: You will find your link here
