How to get a Backlink on mathworks.com
Step 1: Click on the profile icon in the top right corner
Step 2: Open an account
Step 3: Fill in the required fields and click on "Create"
Step 4: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 5: Fill in the required fields
Step 6: Accept the agreement and click on "Create"
Step 7: Select "Edit Community Profile"
Step 8: Fill in "Personal bio" section with a short introduction and your website URL
Step 9: Click on "Update"
Step 10: You will find your link here
