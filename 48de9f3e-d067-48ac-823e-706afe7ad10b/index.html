How to get a Backlink on askmap.net
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Register"
Step 3: Click on "Add a location"
Step 4: Fill in the required fields and click on "Save Changes" (don't forget to enter your website URL)
Step 5: Here is your added location - scroll down the page
Step 6: You will find your link here
