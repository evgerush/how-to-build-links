How to get a Backlink on forumotion.com
Step 1: Create a title and click on "Create a forum"
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Validate"
Step 3: Re-enter your password and click on "Discover My Forum"
Step 4: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 5: Click on "Access the Forum"
Step 6: Log in with your password (leave Username as "Admin")
Step 7: Click on "Profile"
Step 8: Enter your website URL and click on "Save"
Step 9: You will find your link here
