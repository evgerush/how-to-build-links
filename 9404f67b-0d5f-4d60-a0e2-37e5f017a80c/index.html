How to get a Backlink on sitey.com
Step 1: Click on "Get Started"
Step 2: Select or type a category, then click on "Continue"
Step 3: Name your site, then click on "Continue"
Step 4: Click on "Continue"
Step 5: Click on "Continue"
Step 6: Click on "Continue"
Step 7: Click on "Finish"
Step 8: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign up!"
Step 9: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 10: Fill in with short description and your website URL, then click on "Publish"
Step 11: Click on "View Site"
Step 12: You will find your link here
