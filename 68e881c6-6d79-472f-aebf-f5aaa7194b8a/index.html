How to get a Backlink on eventbrite.ie
Step 1: Click on "Create an event"
Step 2: Open an account
Step 3: Enter your email address and click on "Continue"
Step 4: Fill in the required fields and click on "Create account"
Step 5: Agree to terms and conditions
Step 6: Click on "Skip"
Step 7: Add your event details
Step 8: Add tags
Step 9: Set the event as "Online Event" and "Recurring Event", then click on "Save & Continue"
Step 10: In "Details" tab, fill in with your event description and your website URL, then click on "Save & Continue"
Step 11: Set tickets to "Free", then click on "Save"
Step 12: Click on "Publish Now"
Step 13: You will find your link here
