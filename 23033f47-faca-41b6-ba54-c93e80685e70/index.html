How to get a Backlink on prsync.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Send"
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 4: Click on "Submit company"
Step 5: Fill in the required fields and click on "Send" (don't forget to enter your website URL)
Step 6: You will find your link here
