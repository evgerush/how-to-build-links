How to get a Backlink on fileforum.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Create Account"
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 4: Proceed to login screen
Step 5: Login with your credentials
Step 6: Click on "Edit Profile"
Step 7: Fill in with short description and your website URL, then click on "Save Profile"
Step 8: You will find your link here
