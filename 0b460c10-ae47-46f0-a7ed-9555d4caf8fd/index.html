How to get a Backlink on zotero.org
Step 1: Click on "Log In"
Step 2: Open an account
Step 3: Fill in the required fields and click on "Register"
Step 4: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 5: Click on "Edit Profile"
Step 6: Fill in with a short introduction and your website URL (add link using "Link" button), then click on "Save Profile"
Step 7: You will find your link here
