How to get a Backlink on yelp.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign Up"
Step 3: Click on "Skip"
Step 4: Select "Account Settings" in the top right corner
Step 5: Enter your website URL and click on "Save Changes"
Step 6: You will find your link here
