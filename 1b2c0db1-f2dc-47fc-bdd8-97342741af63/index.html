How to get a Backlink on arduino.cc
Step 1: Click on "Sign In"
Step 2: Open an account
Step 3: Enter your birthday, then click on "Next"
Step 4: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign Up"
Step 5: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 6: Click on "Add Personal Info"
Step 7: Enter your website URL and click on "Save" (You can also add a short introduction)
Step 8: You will find your link here
