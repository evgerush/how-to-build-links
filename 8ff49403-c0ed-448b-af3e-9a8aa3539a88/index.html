How to get a Backlink on artstation.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Create account"
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 4: Fill in the required fields and click on "Continue"
Step 5: Choose "Artist"
Step 6: Fill in "Professional Summary" section with a short introduction and your website URL
Step 7: You have to add at least one skill
Step 8: Click on "Continue"
Step 9: You will find your link here
