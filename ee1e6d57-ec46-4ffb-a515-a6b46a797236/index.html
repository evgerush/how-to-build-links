How to get a Backlink on vault.mle.party
Step 1: Go to "https://vault.mle.party/signup" and agree to terms
Step 2: Fill in the required fields, then click on "Next"
Step 3: Create a channel display name and click on "Create my account"
Step 4: Go to your mailbox and click on confirmation link
Step 5: Select "My account" from the left side menu
Step 6: Fill in with short description and your website URL, then click on "Save"
Step 7: You will find your link here
