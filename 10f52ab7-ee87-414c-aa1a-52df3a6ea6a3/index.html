How to get a Backlink on buzzfeed.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Click on "Sign up"
Step 3: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign up"
Step 4: Go to your mailbox and click on activation link from BuzzFeed
Step 5: Click on "Sign In and Get Started"
Step 6: Sign in with your credentials
Step 7: Click on the bell icon and choose "Account Settings"
Step 8: Fill in with your website URL
Step 9: Click on "Save Changes"
Step 10: You will find your link here
