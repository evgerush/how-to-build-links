How to get a Backlink on ted.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Enter your email address and click on "Continue"
Step 3: Create a password and click on "Continue"
Step 4: Fill in with your first and last name, then click on "Continue"
Step 5: Check your mailbox for email with verification link, then click on "Continue"
Step 6: Click on "View profile"
Step 7: Click on "Edit profile"
Step 8: Enter your website URL and name in the "Your links" section
Step 9: Click on "Save"
Step 10: You will find your link here
