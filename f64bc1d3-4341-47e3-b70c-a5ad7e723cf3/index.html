How to get a Backlink on amazon.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Create Your Amazon Account"
Step 3: Check your email for One Time Password, enter it, then click on "Create your Amazon Account"
Step 4: Go to your "Account"
Step 5: Select "Your Amazon profile"
Step 6: Fill in the required fields and click on "Submit"
Step 7: Click on "Edit your public profile"
Step 8: Enter your website URL
Step 9: Click on "Save"
Step 10: You will find your link here
