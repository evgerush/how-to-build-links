How to get a Backlink on e-monsite.com
Step 1: The website is in foreign language with no option to choose language, that’s why we suggest using Chrome browser and translating the page to English
Step 2: Click on "Create a free site"
Step 3: Fill in the required fields and click on "Register now"
Step 4: Fill in the required fields and click on "Save and Choose graphic theme"
Step 5: Choose a template
Step 6: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 7: Select "Manage Pages" in "Pages" submenu
Step 8: Click on "modify" underneath the Home page
Step 9: Fill in with title, short description and your website URL, then click on "Save" (Use "link" button to add link)
Step 10: You will find your link here
