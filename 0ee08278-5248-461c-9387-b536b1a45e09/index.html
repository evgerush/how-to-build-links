How to get a Backlink on triberr.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields
Step 3: Give some more information and click on "Register"
Step 4: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 5: Choose your account "Settings"
Step 6: Fill in "Bio" section with a short introduction and enter your website URL into the "Website" section
Step 7: Click on "Submit"
Step 8: You will find your link here
