How to get a Backlink on setiweb.ssl.berkeley.edu
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Join"
Step 3: Select "Teams" from the top bar
Step 4: Click on "Create a team"
Step 5: Name your team and add a short description, enter your website address(use: www.yoursite.com!) and choose a country, then click on "Create a team"
Step 6: You will find your link here
