How to get a Backlink on affilorama.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Join!"
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 4: Select "profile" in the top right corner
Step 5: Click on the pencil icon
Step 6: Enter your website URL and click on "Save"
Step 7: You will find your link here
