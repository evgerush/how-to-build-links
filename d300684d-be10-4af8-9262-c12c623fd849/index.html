How to get a Backlink on webmasterworld.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Click on "I Agree"
Step 3: Fill in the required fields and click on "Submit"
Step 4: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 5: Log in with your credentials
Step 6: Click on "Control Panel"
Step 7: Select "Edit Profile"
Step 8: Enter your website URL in the "Bio Page URL" section
Step 9: Enter your website URL in the "Company Information" section
Step 10: Click on "Submit"
Step 11: You will find your links here
