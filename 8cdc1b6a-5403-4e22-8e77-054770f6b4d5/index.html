How to get a Backlink on theverge.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign Up"
Step 3: Click on "Sign Up"
Step 4: Click on the link from confirmation email in your mailbox
Step 5: Click on "Edit Profile"
Step 6: Enter your website URL and name, then click on "Update"
Step 7: You will find your link here
