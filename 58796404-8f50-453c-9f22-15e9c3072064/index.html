How to get a Backlink on thegreatapps.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Create Account"
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 4: Log in with your credentials
Step 5: Enter your website URL
Step 6: Fill in the "Description" field with a short introduction
Step 7: Click on "Save"
Step 8: You will find your link here
