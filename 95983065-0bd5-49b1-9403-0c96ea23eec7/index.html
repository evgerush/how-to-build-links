How to get a Backlink on linktr.ee
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields, then click on "Sign up with email"
Step 3: Enter your name and choose 3 categories, then click on "Continue"
Step 4: Choose a free plan
Step 5: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 6: Click on "Add New Link"
Step 7: Enter your website name and URL
Step 8: You will find your link here
