How to get a Backlink on diode.zone
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Agree to the terms and click on "Next"
Step 3: Fill in the required fields, then click on "Next"
Step 4: Create a channel display name and click on "Create my account"
Step 5: Go to your mailbox and click on confirmation link
Step 6: Click on "Login"
Step 7: Login with your credentials
Step 8: Click on "My account"
Step 9: Fill in the "Description" field with short description and your website URL, then click "Save"
Step 10: You will find your link here
