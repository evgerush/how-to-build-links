How to get a Backlink on soundcloud.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Enter your email address and click on "Continue"
Step 3: Create a password and click on "Accept & continue"
Step 4: Fill in with some extra infomation and click on "Continue"
Step 5: Create your display name and click on "Get started"
Step 6: Before proceeding to the next step, check your mailbox and click on the verification link
Step 7: Click on "Profile"
Step 8: Click on "Edit"
Step 9: Select "Add link"
Step 10: Enter your website details and click on "Save changes"
Step 11: You will find your link here
