How to get a Backlink on medium.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Choose to "Sign up with email"
Step 3: Enter your email address and click on "Continue"
Step 4: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 5: Create a username and click on "Create account"
Step 6: Select "Write a story" in the top right corner
Step 7: Fill in with title, short description and your website URL, then click on "Publish"
Step 8: Add tags, then click on "Publish now"
Step 9: Click on "Continue"
Step 10: Click on "Claim URL"
Step 11: Click on "Done"
Step 12: You will find your link here
