How to get a Backlink on visajourney.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Create Account"
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 4: Click on "Validate my account"
Step 5: Select "Profile" in the top right corner
Step 6: Click on "Edit Profile"
Step 7: Click on "link icon" in the "About Me" section
Step 8: Enter your website name and URL, then click on "Insert into post"
Step 9: Click on "Save"
Step 10: You will find your link here
