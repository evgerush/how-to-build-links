How to get a Backlink on bookmarkja.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields
Step 3: Solve captcha, then click on "Create user"
Step 4: Click on "Submit"
Step 5: Enter your website URL, then click on "Continue"
Step 6: Fill in with title, description and add tags
Step 7: Solve captcha, then click on "Save Changes and Submit"
Step 8: You will find your link here
