How to get a Backlink on sbwire.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign Up"
Step 3: You will find your password in your mailbox
Step 4: Sign in with your credentials
Step 5: Click on "professional profile"
Step 6: Click on "Create shared web link"
Step 7: Fill in with a short description, your website URL and name, then click on "Save"
Step 8: You will find your link here
