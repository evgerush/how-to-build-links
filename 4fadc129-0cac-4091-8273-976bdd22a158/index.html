How to get a Backlink on dochub.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign up"
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 4: Click on "Sign In"
Step 5: Sign in with your credentials
Step 6: Click on "Import a document"
Step 7: Choose a document containing your website URL and set privacy to "Public"
Step 8: You will find your link here
