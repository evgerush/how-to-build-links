How to get a Backlink on blogger.com
Step 1: Click on "Sign In"
Step 2: You have to sign in with your Google account
Step 3: Create name for your blog
Step 4: Choose a URL for your blog
Step 5: Create a display name, then click on "Finish"
Step 6: Go to blogger.com/edit-profile.g and fill in with your website URL
Step 7: Fill in "Introduction" with <a href="https://YOURWEBSITE.com">WEBSITE NAME</a> (replace with your website data) and add your website/business description
Step 8: Click on "Save Profile"
Step 9: You will find your links here ( number 1 - No-Follow link; number 2 - Do-Follow link)
