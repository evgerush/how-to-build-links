How to get a Backlink on mytripjournal.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Click on "Start Your Free Journal"
Step 3: Fill in the required fields and click on "Continue"
Step 4: Create URL and title for your journal, then click on "Continue"
Step 5: Click on "Make an Entry"
Step 6: Fill in with title, location, short description and your website URL, then click on "Done" (use link button to enter link)
Step 7: You will find your link here
