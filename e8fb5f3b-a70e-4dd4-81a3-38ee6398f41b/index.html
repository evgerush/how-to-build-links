How to get a Backlink on 4shared.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Click on "Email Address"
Step 3: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign Up"
Step 4: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 5: Select "Settings" in the top right corner
Step 6: Choose "Profile" tab
Step 7: Enter your website URL and click on "Save changes"
Step 8: You will find your link here
