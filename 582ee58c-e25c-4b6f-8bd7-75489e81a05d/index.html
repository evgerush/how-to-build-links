How to get a Backlink on shutterfly.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign Up"
Step 3: Click on "Share Sites"
Step 4: Click on "Create a site"
Step 5: Click on "Make a free site
Step 6: Choose a category. name your site, create URL and set it visible to anyone, then click on "Continue"
Step 7: Choose a template, then click on "Create Site"
Step 8: Click on "Add bookmark"
Step 9: Enter your website URL and select it from the list, then click on "Done"
Step 10: You will find your link here
