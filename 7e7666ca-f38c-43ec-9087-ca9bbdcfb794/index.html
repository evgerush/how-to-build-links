How to get a Backlink on microsoft.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Click on "Create one!"
Step 3: Fill in with your email and click on "Next"
Step 4: Create a password and click on "Next"
Step 5: Fill in with your first and last name, then click on "Next"
Step 6: Choose your Country and set your Birthdate, then click on "Next"
Step 7: Fill in with verification code from your mailbox and click on "Next"
Step 8: Go to answers.microsoft.com and sign in with your account
Step 9: When you are singing in for the 1st time, you should create your display name, accept Microsoft Community Code of Conduct and click on "Finish"
Step 10: Now click on your profile in the upper right corner and select "My Profile"
Step 11: Click on "Edit profile"
Step 12: Put your website URL in the "Link title" and "Link URL" boxes
Step 13: Click on "Save"
Step 14: You will find your link here
