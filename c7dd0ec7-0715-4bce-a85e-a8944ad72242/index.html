How to get a Backlink on completed.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Create your account
Step 3: Log in your account
Step 4: Use your credentials to log in
Step 5: Click Menu on the top left corner
Step 6: Click "Edit my Completed.com profile"
Step 7: Click "+ Edit"
Step 8: Submit your Link in the field "Hire Me"
Step 9: Verify your email
Step 10: Search for your name
Step 11: You will find your link here
