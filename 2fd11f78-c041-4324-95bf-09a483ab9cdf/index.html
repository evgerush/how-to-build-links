How to get a Backlink on issuu.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Click on "Get Started" under the Basic (Free) plan
Step 3: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign Up"
Step 4: Enter the verification code from your mailbox and click on "Validate"
Step 5: Click on "Public Profile" in the upper right corner
Step 6: Click on "Edit Profile"
Step 7: Enter your website URL
Step 8: Click on "Save Changes"
Step 9: You will find your link here
