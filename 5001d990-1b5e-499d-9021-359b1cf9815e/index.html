How to get a Backlink on youtube.com
Step 1: Click on "Sign In"
Step 2: Sign in with your Google account
Step 3: Select "Create a channel"
Step 4: Name your channel and click on "Create Channel"
Step 5: Select "About" tab, then click on "Customize Channel"
Step 6: Click on "Add Link" in the "Basic info" tab
Step 7: Enter your website URL and name, then click on "Publish"
Step 8: You will find your link here
