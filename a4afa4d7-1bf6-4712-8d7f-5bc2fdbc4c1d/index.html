How to get a Backlink on crunchyroll.com
Step 1: Click on "Login"
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Create Account"
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 4: Choose "Settings" in the top right corner
Step 5: Select "Basic Information"
Step 6: Click on "link icon" in the "About Me" section
Step 7: Enter your website URL and click on "OK"
Step 8: Click on "Save Changes"
Step 9: You will find your link here
