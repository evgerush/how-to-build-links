How to get a Backlink on vivoldi.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Enter your email address and click on "Request auth number"
Step 3: Get your authentication code from your mailbox
Step 4: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign Up"
Step 5: Select "Create link" from the menu
Step 6: Enter your website URL and click on "Save"
Step 7: Select "Lists" from the "Links" section of the menu
Step 8: You will find your link here
