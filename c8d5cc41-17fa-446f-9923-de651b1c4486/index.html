How to get a Backlink on localendar.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Create My Account"
Step 3: Click on "Add Event"
Step 4: Enter title, fill in the "Descrioption" field with a short description and your website URL(use link button), then click on "Save"
Step 5: You will find your link here
