How to get a Backlink on evernote.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Choose free option
Step 3: Fill in the required fields and click on "Continue"
Step 4: Fill in with title, short description and your website URL, then click on "Share" (Use "link" button to add link)
Step 5: Enable shareable link and copy it, then open it in your browser
Step 6: You will find your link here
