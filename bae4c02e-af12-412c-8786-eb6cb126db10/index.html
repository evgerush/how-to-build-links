How to get a Backlink on votetags.com
Step 1: Click on "Login/Register"
Step 2: Choose to "Register"
Step 3: Fill in the required fields and click on "Register"
Step 4: Click on "Submit Article"
Step 5: Fill in with title, description, enter tags, choose a category, then click on "Preview & Submit"
Step 6: You will find your link here
