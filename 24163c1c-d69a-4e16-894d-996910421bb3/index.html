How to get a Backlink on findery.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign me up!"
Step 3: Sign in with your credentials
Step 4: Select "Settings" in the top right corner
Step 5: Enter your website URL and click on "Save settings"
Step 6: You will find your link here
