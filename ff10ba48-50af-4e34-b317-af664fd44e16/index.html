How to get a Backlink on visual.ly
Step 1: Click on "Login"
Step 2: Open an account
Step 3: Fill in the required fields and click on "Submit"
Step 4: Before proceeding to the next step, check your mailbox and click on the verification link
Step 5: Click on "Profile"
Step 6: Select "Edit Profile"
Step 7: Enter your website URL
Step 8: Click on "Save Profile"
Step 9: You will find your link here
