How to get a Backlink on docdroid.net
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Register"
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 4: Upload your PDF file that contains your website URL
Step 5: Open the provided link
Step 6: You will find your link here
