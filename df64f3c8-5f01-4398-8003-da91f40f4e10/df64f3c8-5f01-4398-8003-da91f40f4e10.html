How to get a Backlink on stackoverflow.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign up"
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 4: Click on "Create my account" (You can upload profile picture if you want to)
Step 5: Fill in the required fields and click on "Continue"
Step 6: Fill in the required fields and click on "Done"
Step 7: Click on "Edit profile and settings"
Step 8: Enter your website URL and click on "Save profile"
Step 9: You will find your link here
