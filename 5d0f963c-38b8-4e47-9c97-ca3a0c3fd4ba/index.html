How to get a Backlink on sitejot.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Submit" (check "Make your bookmarks public" box)
Step 3: Click on "Add Site"
Step 4: Enter your website URL, add short description and select a category, then click on "Add"
Step 5: Click on "Public Bookmarks"
Step 6: Enter your username and click on "Submit"
Step 7: You will find your link here
