How to get a Backlink on magcloud.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign Up"
Step 3: Check your mailbox for email from MagCloud
Step 4: Click on the "Confirm Account" button from confirmation email in your mailbox
Step 5: Click on "Login"
Step 6: Log in with your credentials
Step 7: Click on "My Account"
Step 8: Select "Edit"
Step 9: Fill in "Public Bio" section with <a href="https://YOURWEBSITE.com">YOUR WEBSITE NAME</a> (replace with your website data) and enter your website URL into the "Website" section, then "Save Changes"
Step 10: You will find your links here ( number 1 - Do-Follow link; number 2 - No-Follow link)
