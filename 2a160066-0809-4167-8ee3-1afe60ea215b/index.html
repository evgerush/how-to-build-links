How to get a Backlink on huntingnet.com
Step 1: Go to https://www.huntingnet.com/forum/ and click on "Register"
Step 2: Fill in the required fields
Step 3: Click on "Complete Registration"
Step 4: To activate your account you need to go to your mailbox
Step 5: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 6: Click on "Edit my profile details"
Step 7: Enter your website URL
Step 8: Click on "Save Changes"
Step 9: You will find your link here
