How to get a Backlink on forums.tomsguide.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields
Step 3: Agree to the terms and click on "Register"
Step 4: Go to your mailbox and click on confirmation link
Step 5: Click on "Edit your account details"
Step 6: Fill in with short description and your website URL, then click on "Save" (use "link" button to insert link"
Step 7: You will find your link here
