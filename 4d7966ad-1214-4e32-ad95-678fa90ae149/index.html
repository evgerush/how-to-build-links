How to get a Backlink on wordpress.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Create your account"
Step 3: Create a domain name related to your website and choose "Free" option
Step 4: Click on "start with a free site"
Step 5: Click on "Name your site"
Step 6: Create Site title, then click on "All done, continue"
Step 7: Click on "Save settings"
Step 8: Click on "Visit site"
Step 9: Select "Launch site"
Step 10: Select "Pages" from the left side menu
Step 11: Edit your Homepage
Step 12: Fill in your Homepage with short description and your website URL, then click on "Update"
Step 13: You will find your link here
