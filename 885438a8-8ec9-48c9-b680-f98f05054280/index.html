How to get a Backlink on sketchfab.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Create Account"
Step 3: Fill in with verification code from your mailbox
Step 4: Select one from the options - no matter which one you will choose
Step 5: Select "Individual", then click on "Continue"
Step 6: Select "Settings" in the top right corner
Step 7: Enter your website URL
Step 8: Click on "Save Profile"
Step 9: You will find your link here
