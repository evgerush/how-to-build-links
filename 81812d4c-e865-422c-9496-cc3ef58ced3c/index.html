How to get a Backlink on webgarden.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Create your website name, then click on "Next"
Step 3: Fill in the required fields and click on "Next"
Step 4: Click on "Edit website"
Step 5: Fill in with title and short description
Step 6: Click on the "+" icon
Step 7: Select "Link"
Step 8: Enter your website name and URL, then click on "Save"
Step 9: You will find your link here
