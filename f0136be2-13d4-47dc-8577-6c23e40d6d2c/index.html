How to get a Backlink on intensedebate.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign up"
Step 3: Go to your mailbox
Step 4: Click on the activation link
Step 5: Click on "Edit your profile"
Step 6: Click on "Add Blog / Website"
Step 7: Enter your website details and click on "Save Settings"
Step 8: You will find your link here
