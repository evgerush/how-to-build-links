How to get a Backlink on toolbox.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Click on "Register"
Step 3: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign Up"
Step 4: Go to your mailbox and click on "Start exploring the site!"
Step 5: Sign in with your credentials
Step 6: Click on your profile picture
Step 7: Click on "Edit Your Profile"
Step 8: Enter your website URL
Step 9: Click on "Update Profile"
Step 10: You will find your link here
