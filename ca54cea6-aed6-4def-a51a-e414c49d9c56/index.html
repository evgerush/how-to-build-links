How to get a Backlink on discourse.getgrav.org
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Create your account"
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on confirmation link
Step 4: Click on "Click here to activate your account"
Step 5: Click on "New Topic"
Step 6: Fill in with title, short description and your website URL, select a category, then click on "Create Topic" (use "link" icon to insert link)
Step 7: You will find your link here
