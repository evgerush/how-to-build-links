How to get a Backlink on ibm.com
Step 1: Click on "My IBM" in the top right corner
Step 2: Click on "Create an IBMid"
Step 3: Fill in the requred fields and click on "Next"
Step 4: Fill in with verification code from your mailbox
Step 5: Click on "Create account"
Step 6: Click on "Proceed"
Step 7: Click on "Log in"
Step 8: Fill in with your email and click on "Continue"
Step 9: Enter your password and "Log in"
Step 10: Select "IBM Community Profile" in the top right corner
Step 11: Edit your contact details
Step 12: Fill in with your website URL
Step 13: Click on "Save"
Step 14: You will find your link here
