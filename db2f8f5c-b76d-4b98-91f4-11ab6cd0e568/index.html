How to get a Backlink on goodreads.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign up"
Step 3: Skip the next step
Step 4: Skip this step also
Step 5: Select at least one genre to be able to continue
Step 6: Click on "I'm finished rating"
Step 7: Click on "Profile"
Step 8: Select "edit profile"
Step 9: Enter your website URL
Step 10: Click on "Save Profile Settings"
Step 11: You will find your link here
