How to get a Backlink on redbubble.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Choose the "Customer signup", fill in the required fields and click on "Sign Up"
Step 3: Select "Account Settings" in the top right corner
Step 4: Fill in "Bio" section with a short introduction and your website URL
Step 5: Click on "Save Changes"
Step 6: You will find your link here
