How to get a Backlink on supportduweb.com
Step 1: Click on the designed button to open an account
Step 2: Fill in with username(1), password(2) and your email address(3), then click on the designed button
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 4: Log in with your credentials
Step 5: Select "Mon compte" in the top right corner
Step 6: Choose "Modifier mon profil"
Step 7: Enter your website URL (add link using "Link" button), then click on "Modifier"
Step 8: You will find your link here
