How to get a Backlink on livejournal.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Create Account"
Step 3: Select "Profile" in the top right corner
Step 4: Click on "Edit Profile"
Step 5: Fill in "BIO" section with <a href="https://YOURWEBSITE.com">WEBSITE NAME</a> (replace with your website data) and enter your website URL in the "WEB PAGE" section
Step 6: Click on "Save Changes"
Step 7: You will find your link here
