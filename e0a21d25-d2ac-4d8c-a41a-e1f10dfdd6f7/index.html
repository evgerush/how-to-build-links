How to get a Backlink on files.fm
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign up"
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 4: Fill in the required fields and click on "Save information"
Step 5: Go to "My public profile settings"
Step 6: In the "Description" tab, fill in with a short introduction and your website URL then click on "Save settings" (Use a "link" button to insetr link)
Step 7: You will find your link here
