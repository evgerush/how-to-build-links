How to get a Backlink on shrunken.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Register"
Step 3: You will find your password in your mailbox
Step 4: Login with your credentials
Step 5: Click on "Click here"
Step 6: Enter your website URL and click on "Create shrunken URL!"
Step 7: You will find your link here
