How to get a Backlink on mappery.com
Step 1: Click on "Sign Up To Create Your Room"
Step 2: Click on "Sign up for your own map room"
Step 3: Fill in the required fields and click on "Register"
Step 4: Click on "My Map Room"
Step 5: Click on "Edit My Account"
Step 6: Enter your website URL and click on "Save"
Step 7: You will find your link here
