How to get a Backlink on dotnetkicks.com
Step 1: Click on "Login"
Step 2: Login with your Google account
Step 3: Enter your Twitter account (or choose that you don't have one - you will add it later), fill in with a short description and your email address, answer the questions as shown, then click on "Join us"
Step 4: Select "My Profile" in the top right corner
Step 5: Click on the pencil icon
Step 6: Enter your website URL and your Twitter handle, then click on "Save"
Step 7: You will find your link here
