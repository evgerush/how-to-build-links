How to get a Backlink on 1minutesite.co.uk
Step 1: Click on "Sign up"
Step 2: Open an account
Step 3: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign up"
Step 4: Select a template
Step 5: Fill in with title, short description and your website URL, then click on "Publish"
Step 6: Click on "Free Version"
Step 7: Create a website name, then click on "Save"
Step 8: You will find your link here
