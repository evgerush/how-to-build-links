How to get a Backlink on moonfruit.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign me up"
Step 3: Create site name and address, then click on "Submit"
Step 4: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 5: From the "Sections" tab choose a template
Step 6: Fill in with title and short description, then click on the "Like things now" button
Step 7: Enter your website URL
Step 8: Rename the button
Step 9: Click on "Save"
Step 10: Click on "Publish"
Step 11: Click on "Continue for free"
Step 12: Click on "Continue"
Step 13: You will find your link here
