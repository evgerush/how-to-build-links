How to get a Backlink on padlet.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign Up"
Step 3: Choose free plan and click on "Continue"
Step 4: Click on "Let's go"
Step 5: Click on "Make a Padlet"
Step 6: Click on "Select" on the Wall option
Step 7: Click on "+" icon
Step 8: Enter title and click on the "link" icon
Step 9: Enter your website URL and proceed
Step 10: Click on "Publish"
Step 11: You will find your link here
