How to get a Backlink on twitch.tv
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign Up"
Step 3: Enter the verification code from your mailbox and click on "Submit"
Step 4: Select at least 1 interest to continue
Step 5: Select "Channel" in the top right corner
Step 6: Click on "Edit about & links"
Step 7: Enter your website name and URL, then click on "Add"
Step 8: You will find your link here
