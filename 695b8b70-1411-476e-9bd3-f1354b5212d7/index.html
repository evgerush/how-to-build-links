How to get a Backlink on flipgorilla.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Agree and Continue"
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 4: Login with your credentials
Step 5: Click on "Create New"
Step 6: Upload your PDF file that contains your website URL
Step 7: Fill in with title and short description, then click on "Create"
Step 8: You will find your link here
