How to get a Backlink on publiclab.org
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign up" - enter your website URL and short description into the "Bio" field
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 4: You will find your link here
