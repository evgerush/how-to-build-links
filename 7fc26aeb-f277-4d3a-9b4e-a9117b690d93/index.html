How to get a Backlink on devtube.dev-wiki.de
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Agree to terms and click on "Next"
Step 3: Fill in the required fields and click on "Next"
Step 4: Create a channel display name and click on "Create my account"
Step 5: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 6: Click on "Login"
Step 7: Login with your credentials
Step 8: Select "Public Profile" in the top left corner
Step 9: Click on "Manage account"
Step 10: Fill in with a short description and your website URL, then click on "Save"
Step 11: You will find your link here
