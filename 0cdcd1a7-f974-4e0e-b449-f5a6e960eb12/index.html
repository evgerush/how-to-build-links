How to get a Backlink on us.webnode.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Register & Create"
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 4: Click on your website
Step 5: Choose to "Activate project"
Step 6: Choose "Website"
Step 7: Choose a theme
Step 8: Click on "Edit my site"
Step 9: Fill in with title and short description, then click on the gear icon
Step 10: Enable "Button"
Step 11: Click on "Edit link"
Step 12: Fill in with your website URL and press "Enter" on your keyboard
Step 13: Rename the button
Step 14: Click on "Publish"
Step 15: You will find your link here
