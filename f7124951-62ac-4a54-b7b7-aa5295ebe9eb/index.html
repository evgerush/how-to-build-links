How to get a Backlink on calendly.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Enter your email address and click on "Sign Up"
Step 3: Select to create an account with a password
Step 4: Fill in the required fields and click on "Continue"
Step 5: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 6: Enter your password and "Continue"
Step 7: Click on "Continue"
Step 8: Click on "Continue without calendar"
Step 9: Click on "Continue"
Step 10: Choose "Freelance + Consulting", then click on "Continue"
Step 11: Click on "Finish"
Step 12: Create "New Event Type"
Step 13: Click on "Create"
Step 14: Fill in with event name and short description with your website URL (use link icon to enable link), then click on "Next"
Step 15: Click on "Next"
Step 16: You will find your link here
