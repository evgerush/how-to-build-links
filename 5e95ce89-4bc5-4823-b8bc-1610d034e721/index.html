How to get a Backlink on affiliatefix.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Register"
Step 3: Select "Account details" in the top right corner
Step 4: Enter your website URL and click on "Save"
Step 5: You will find your link here
