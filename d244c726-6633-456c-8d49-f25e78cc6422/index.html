How to get a Backlink on fan.school
Step 1: Sign in with your Google account
Step 2: Click on "Skip"
Step 3: Click on "Write an article"
Step 4: Fill in with title, short description and your website URL, then click on "Publish" (Use "link" button to add link)
Step 5: You will find your link here
