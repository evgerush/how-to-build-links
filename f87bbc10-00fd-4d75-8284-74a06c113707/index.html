How to get a Backlink on wakelet.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Choose to sign up with email
Step 3: Fill in the required fields and click on "Create your account"
Step 4: Click on "Get Started"
Step 5: Select one and click on "Next"
Step 6: Click on "View Public Profile"
Step 7: Click on "Edit Profile"
Step 8: Click on the "+" icon
Step 9: Enter your website URL and click on "Save"
Step 10: Click on "Save"
Step 11: You will find your link here
