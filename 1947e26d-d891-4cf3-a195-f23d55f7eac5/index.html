How to get a Backlink on myblogu.com
Step 1: Fill in the required fields and click on "Join FREE"
Step 2: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 3: Sign in with your credentials
Step 4: Select "Profile" in the top right corner
Step 5: Click on "Add Signature"
Step 6: Fill in with a short introduction and your website URL (add link using "Link" button), select to include this section in your public profile, then click on "Update profile"
Step 7: You will find your link here
