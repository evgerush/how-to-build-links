How to get a Backlink on speakerdeck.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign up"
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 4: Click on "Edit my profile"
Step 5: Fill in "Bio" section with your website URL, then click on "Update profile"
Step 6: You will find your link here
