How to get a Backlink on forums.bestbuy.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields
Step 3: Accept the terms of service, enter your name, set your time zone and click on "Register"
Step 4: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 5: Click on "My Settings"
Step 6: Enter your website URL in the "Personal Information" tab
Step 7: Click on "Save"
Step 8: You will find your link here
