How to get a Backlink on gappoo.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Create New Account"
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 4: Create a password and click on "Save"
Step 5: Select "Add" in the top right corner
Step 6: Fill in with title, short description and your website URL, select a category (Use "link" button to add link)
Step 7: Enter your website name and URL, then click on "Save"
Step 8: You will find your link here
