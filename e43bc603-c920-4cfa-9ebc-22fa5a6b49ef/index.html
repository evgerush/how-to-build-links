How to get a Backlink on permaculturenews.org
Step 1: Go to https://permaculturenews.org/forums/ and click on "Login/Sign up"
Step 2: Enter your email address, choose to create an account and click on "Sign Up"
Step 3: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign Up"
Step 4: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 5: Click on "Edit your account details"
Step 6: Enter your website URL and click on "Save Changes"
Step 7: You will find your link here
