How to get a Backlink on opendiary.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign Up For a New Account"
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 4: Click on "log in"
Step 5: Log in with your credentials
Step 6: Click on the designated icon to add an entry
Step 7: Fill in with title, short description and your website URL, then click on "Publish"
Step 8: You will find your link here
