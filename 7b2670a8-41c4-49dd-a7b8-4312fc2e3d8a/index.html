How to get a Backlink on creativemarket.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Choose to sign up with email
Step 3: Fill in the required fields and click on "Create Account"
Step 4: Select "Settings" in the top right corner
Step 5: Enter your website URL
Step 6: Click on "Update Settings"
Step 7: You will find your link here
