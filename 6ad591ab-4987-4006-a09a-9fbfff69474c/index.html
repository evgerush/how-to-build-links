How to get a Backlink on giantbomb.com
Step 1: Click on "Login/Sign Up"
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and select "Basic - Free" account type
Step 3: Click on "Create account"
Step 4: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 5: Select "Blog" in the top right corner
Step 6: Click on "Create a new blog entry"
Step 7: Fill in with a short introduction and your website URL (add link using "Link" button), then click on "Save your blog"
Step 8: You will find your link here
