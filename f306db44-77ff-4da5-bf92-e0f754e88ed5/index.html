How to get a Backlink on oercommons.org
Step 1: Click on "Sign In/Register"
Step 2: Open an account
Step 3: Fill in the required fields and click on "Register"
Step 4: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 5: Select "Profile Information" in the top right corner
Step 6: Select "Create a Group"
Step 7: Name your group and click on "Next: Describe"
Step 8: Choose subjects and grade of your group, then click on "Next: Describe"
Step 9: Fill in with a short description and your website URL, then click on "Next: Permissions"
Step 10: Choose, how people can join your group and click on "Next: Create My Group!"
Step 11: You will find your link here
