How to get a Backlink on flaticon.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign up"
Step 3: Select "Edit Profile" in the top right corner
Step 4: Enter your website URL and click on "Save changes"
Step 5: You will find your link here
