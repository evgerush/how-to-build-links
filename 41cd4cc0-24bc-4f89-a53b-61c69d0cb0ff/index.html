How to get a Backlink on bidinc.org
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Register" - don't forget to enter your website URL
Step 3: Log in with your credentials
Step 4: Click on "Submit Post"
Step 5: Fill in with title, tags, short description, your website URL, choose category, then click on "Submit Post"
Step 6: You will find your link here
