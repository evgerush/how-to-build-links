How to get a Backlink on cabanova.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign Up!"
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 4: Select "My Sites" in the top right corner
Step 5: Click on "Edit site"
Step 6: Choose a template
Step 7: Fill in with title, short description and your website URL (Use "link" button to add link)
Step 8: Select "Publish online" in the top left corner
Step 9: You will find your link here
