How to get a Backlink on giphy.com
Step 1: Click on "Log in"
Step 2: Fill in the required fields in "Sign Up" tab and click on "Sign Up"
Step 3: Select "Settings" in the top right corner
Step 4: Make sure that your channel is "Public", enter your website URL and click on "Save"
Step 5: You will find your link here
