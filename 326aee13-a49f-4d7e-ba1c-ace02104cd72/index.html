How to get a Backlink on edublogs.org
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields, then click on "Create a Site"
Step 3: You will find login link in your mailbox - click on it
Step 4: Enter some additional information and click on "Continue Blogging"
Step 5: Click on "New"
Step 6: Fill in with title, short description and your website URL, then click on "Publish"
Step 7: You will find your link here
