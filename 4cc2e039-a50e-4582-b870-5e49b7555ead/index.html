How to get a Backlink on nextbizthing.com
Step 1: Click on "Get Listed Today"
Step 2: Select a free option
Step 3: Fill in the required fields and click on "Create My Profile"
Step 4: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 5: Fill in the required fields
Step 6: Enter your website URL and click on "Save & Continue"
Step 7: Upload Logo and click on "Continue to Next Section"
Step 8: Fill in with short description and click on "Save & Continue"
Step 9: Fill in with a description and click on "Save & Contiune"
Step 10: Click on "Back to Dashboard"
Step 11: You will find your link here
