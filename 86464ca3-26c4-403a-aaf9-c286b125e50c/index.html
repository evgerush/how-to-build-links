How to get a Backlink on wantedly.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Join Now"
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 4: Select "Update your profile" in the top right corner
Step 5: Click on "Next"
Step 6: Click on "Go to your profile"
Step 7: Click on "Profile"
Step 8: Enter short description(1), then click on "Add link"(2)
Step 9: Enter your website URL, set it to "Public", then click on "Save"
Step 10: You will find your link here
