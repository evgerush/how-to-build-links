How to get a Backlink on aytoloja.org
Step 1: Go to "https://www/aytoloja.org/jforum/user/insert.page"
Step 2: The website is in foreign language with no option to choose language, that’s why we suggest using Chrome browser and translating the page to English
Step 3: Accept the terms
Step 4: Fill in the required fields and click on "Send"
Step 5: Click on "here"
Step 6: Fill in the "Biography" section with short description and "<a href="https://YOURWEBSITE.com">WEBSITE NAME</a>" (replace with your website data), then click on "Send"
Step 7: You will find your link here
