How to get a Backlink on bookmarkspot.com
Step 1: Click on "Submit Story"
Step 2: Click on "Login OR Register"
Step 3: Fill in the required fields and click on "Register"
Step 4: Log in with your credentials
Step 5: Click on "Submit Story"
Step 6: Fill in the required fields and click on "Submit" - don't forget to enter your website URL
Step 7: You will find your link here
