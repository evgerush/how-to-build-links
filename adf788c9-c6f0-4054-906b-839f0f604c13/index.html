How to get a Backlink on tumblr.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign up"
Step 3: Fill in with your age and click on "Next"
Step 4: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 5: Go to your dashboard
Step 6: Click on the pencil icon
Step 7: Select "Link"
Step 8: Enter your website URL
Step 9: Click on "Post"
Step 10: You will find your link here
