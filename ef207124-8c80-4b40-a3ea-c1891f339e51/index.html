How to get a Backlink on studiopress.community
Step 1: Go to https://studiopress.community and Open an account
Step 2: Create your account
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and look for message from Studiopress
Step 4: Visit the link provided by Studiopress to set your password
Step 5: Create your password and save it
Step 6: Click on "Log in"
Step 7: Fill in with your credentials and log in
Step 8: Click on your account picture in the upper right corner
Step 9: Click on "Edit"
Step 10: Fill in "Biographical Info" with <a href="https://YOURWEBSITE.com">WEBSITE NAME</a> (replace with your website data) and add your website/business description
Step 11: Click on "Update Profile"
Step 12: You will find your link here
