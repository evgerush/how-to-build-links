How to get a Backlink on myspace.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Create Account"
Step 3: Click on "Take me to my Profile"
Step 4: Click on "Verify Email" button that you will find in the email from Myspace
Step 5: Go to https://myspace.com/settings/profile/email and click on "Edit Profile"
Step 6: Enter your website URL and press "Enter" on your keyboard
Step 7: You will find your link here
