How to get a Backlink on dpreview.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Create your DPReview account"
Step 3: You will find your OTP (One Time Password) in your mailbox, enter it and click on "Create your DPReview account"
Step 4: Create a username and click on "Create account"
Step 5: Select "Settings" in the top right corner
Step 6: Enter your website URL and click on "Save Changes"
Step 7: You will find your link here
