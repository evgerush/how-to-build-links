How to get a Backlink on tripadvisor.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Click on "Continue with email"
Step 3: Click on "Join"
Step 4: Fill in the required fields and click on "Join"
Step 5: Select "View profile" in the top right corner
Step 6: Click on "Edit profile"
Step 7: Enter your website URL and click on "Save"
Step 8: You will find your link here
