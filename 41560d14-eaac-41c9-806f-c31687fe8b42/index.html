How to get a Backlink on webhitlist.com
Step 1: Click on "Sign In"
Step 2: Open an account
Step 3: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign Up"
Step 4: Fill in the required fields and click on "Join"
Step 5: Go to your mailbox and click on the link to "Set up your profile"
Step 6: Fill in with short description and your website URL, then click on "Share"
Step 7: You will find your link here
