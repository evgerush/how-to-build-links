How to get a Backlink on sourceforge.net
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Register"
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 4: Click on "Create a project of your own"
Step 5: Login with your credentials
Step 6: Create Project Name and URL Name (those should be automatically filled with your username)
Step 7: Choose "Blog", then click on "Create"
Step 8: Enter your phone number, then click on "Submit"
Step 9: Fill in with PIN that you should recieve to your phone number, then click on "Submit"
Step 10: Click on "Blog"
Step 11: Click on "New Post"
Step 12: Fill in with title, short description and your website URL, then click on "Save"
Step 13: You will find your link here
