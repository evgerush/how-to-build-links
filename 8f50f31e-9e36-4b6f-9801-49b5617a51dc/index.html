How to get a Backlink on vimeo.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Join with email"
Step 3: Click on "Skip"
Step 4: Select "No thanks"
Step 5: Click on "Edit profile"
Step 6: Fill in "About" section with short introduction and your website URL
Step 7: Click on "Save"
Step 8: You will find your link here
