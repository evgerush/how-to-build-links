How to get a Backlink on launchora.com
Step 1: Click on "Sign In"
Step 2: Choose to "Continue with Google"
Step 3: Select "Pencil icon" in the top right corner
Step 4: Fill in with title, short description and your website URL, then click on "Publish" (Use "link" button to add link)
Step 5: Add a description and pick a collection (category), then click on "Publish"
Step 6: You will find your link here
