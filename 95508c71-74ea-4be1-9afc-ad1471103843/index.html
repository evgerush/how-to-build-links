How to get a Backlink on viki.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Click on "Continue with Email"
Step 3: Fill in the required fields and click on "Continue"
Step 4: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 5: Select "Profile" in the top right corner
Step 6: Click on "Edit Profile"
Step 7: Select "About Me" tab
Step 8: Fill in with a short introduction and your website URL, select the URL, then click on "Link" icon
Step 9: Enter your website URL and click on "OK"
Step 10: Click on "Save"
Step 11: You will find your link here
