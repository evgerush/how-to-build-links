How to get a Backlink on loop.frontiersin.org
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in with your email address and click on "Continue"
Step 3: Fill in the required fields and click on "Register"
Step 4: Click on "Continue" and check your mailbox
Step 5: Click on the "Activate account" button that you will find in your mailbox
Step 6: Click on "Bio"
Step 7: Click on "Add your blog or website"
Step 8: Enter your website URL and click on "Save"
Step 9: You will find your link here
