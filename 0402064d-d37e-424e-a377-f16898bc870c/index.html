How to get a Backlink on carrd.co
Step 1: Click on menu in the top right corner
Step 2: Open an account
Step 3: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign Up"
Step 4: Click on "Send Verification Email"
Step 5: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 6: Click on "Verify Account"
Step 7: Click on "New Site"
Step 8: Select a template
Step 9: Fill in with title, short description and your website URL, add picture, then click on "Done" (To insert link, type "[YOURWEBSITENAME](yoursiteURL)" - replace with your website data)
Step 10: Click on save icon
Step 11: Fill in the required fields and click on "Publish"
Step 12: You will find your link here
