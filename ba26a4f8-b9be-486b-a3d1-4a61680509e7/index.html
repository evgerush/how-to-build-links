How to get a Backlink on openfaves.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Click on "Register"
Step 3: Fill in the required fields and click on "Register"
Step 4: Click on "Add Article"
Step 5: Enter your website URL, create a Title, select category, enter tags, give a short description, then click on "Preview & Submit"
Step 6: You will find your link here
