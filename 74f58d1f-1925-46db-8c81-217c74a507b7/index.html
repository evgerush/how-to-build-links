How to get a Backlink on mru.org
Step 1: Click on "My Account"
Step 2: Select "Create new account"
Step 3: Fill in the required fields and click on "Create New Account" - don't forget to enter your website URL
Step 4: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 5: Click on "Log In"
Step 6: Create a password
Step 7: Click on "Save"
Step 8: Fill in "About" section with a short introduction and your website URL
Step 9: Click on "Save"
Step 10: You will find your link here
