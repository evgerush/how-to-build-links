How to get a Backlink on change.org
Step 1: Click on "Start a petition"
Step 2: Select a topic, then click on "Continue"
Step 3: Fill in with a short description, then click on "Continue"
Step 4: You have to enter the target audience - you can use "Entrepreneurs", then click on "Continue"
Step 5: Fill in with a short introduction and your website URL (add link using "Link" button), then click on "Continue"
Step 6: Upload a picture and click on "Save and preview"
Step 7: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign up"
Step 8: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 9: Create a password and click on "Continue"
Step 10: Click on "Publish"
Step 11: You will find your link here
