How to get a Backlink on simbla.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Start building websites!"
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 4: Choose to "Create new" "Website"
Step 5: Use a template
Step 6: Choose a template
Step 7: Name your website and click on "Save"
Step 8: Click on "Skip"
Step 9: Fill in with title, description and your website URL (Use "link" button to add link)
Step 10: Click on "Publish"
Step 11: You will find your link here
