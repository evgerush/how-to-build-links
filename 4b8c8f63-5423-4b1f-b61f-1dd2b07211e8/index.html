How to get a Backlink on journoportfolio.com
Step 1: Click on "Create a Portfolio"
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Get Started"
Step 3: Select theme for your portfolio
Step 4: Name your portfolio, fill in "Profile Bio" section with a short introduction and your website URL (add link using "Link" button), then click on "Next"
Step 5: Click on "Next"
Step 6: Click on "Skip"
Step 7: You will find your link here
