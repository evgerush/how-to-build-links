How to get a Backlink on zeef.com
Step 1: Click on "Create a page"
Step 2: Name your page and click on "Continue"
Step 3: Click on "Create a page"
Step 4: Choose to sign up with email
Step 5: Fill in the required fields, then click on "Continue"
Step 6: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 7: Go to "Your ZEEF page"
Step 8: Click on "Links"
Step 9: Fill in with title and your website URL, then click on "+"
Step 10: Click on "Publish"
Step 11: You will find your link here
