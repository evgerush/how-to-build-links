How to get a Backlink on linklyhq.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign Up"
Step 3: Click on "Create my first link"
Step 4: Enter your website URL and title, then click on "Save Link"
Step 5: You will find your link here
