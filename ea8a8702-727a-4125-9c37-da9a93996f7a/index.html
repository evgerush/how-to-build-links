How to get a Backlink on doodlekit.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Create My Website Now!"
Step 3: Fill in with title, short description and your website URL by editing designated sections
Step 4: Click on "Publish Site"
Step 5: Select "Publish Free"
Step 6: You will find your link here
