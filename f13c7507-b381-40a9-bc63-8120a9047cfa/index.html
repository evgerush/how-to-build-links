How to get a Backlink on domestika.org
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign Up"
Step 3: Choose at least one category and click on "Continue"
Step 4: Click on "Continue"
Step 5: Click on "Follow all and continue"
Step 6: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 7: Select "Account settings" in the top right corner
Step 8: Enter your website URL and click on "Save changes"
Step 9: You will find your link here
