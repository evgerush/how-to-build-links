How to get a Backlink on tribalpages.com
Step 1: Click on "Join Now!"
Step 2: Fill in the required fields, then click on "Sign Me Up"
Step 3: Create your website ID(username) and choose your location, then click on "Create Website"
Step 4: Proceed to your website
Step 5: Select "Edit"
Step 6: Login with your email and password
Step 7: Select "Homepage" in the top left corner
Step 8: Click on "Edit" next to the "Useful Links" section
Step 9: Enter your website name and URL, then click on "Save"
Step 10: You will find your link here
