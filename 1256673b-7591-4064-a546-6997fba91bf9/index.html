How to get a Backlink on fxstat.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign Up"
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 4: Click on your profile picture
Step 5: Click on "Add Article"
Step 6: Fill in with short introduction and your website URL (use link button to enable link), then click on "Publish"
Step 7: You will find your link here
