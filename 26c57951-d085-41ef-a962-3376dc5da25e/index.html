How to get a Backlink on joemonster.org
Step 1: The website is in foreign language with no option to choose language, that’s why we suggest using Chrome browser and translating the page to English
Step 2: Open an account
Step 3: Fill in the required fields and click on "Create an Account"
Step 4: You will find your password in your mailbox
Step 5: Login with your credentials
Step 6: Fill in the "Signature" field with short description and your website URL, then click on "Save changes"
Step 7: You will find your link here
