How to get a Backlink on linkarena.com
Step 1: The website is in foreign language with no option to choose language, that’s why we suggest using Chrome browser and translating the page to English
Step 2: Open an account
Step 3: Fill in the required fields and click on "Register"
Step 4: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 5: Login with your credentials
Step 6: Click on "Add bookmark"
Step 7: Enter your website URL and click on "Continue"
Step 8: Add title, description and tags, name your folder, then click on "Continue"
Step 9: You will find your link here
