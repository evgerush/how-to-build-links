How to get a Backlink on debate.org
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign Up"
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 4: Click on "Profile" on the left side panel
Step 5: Click on "Edit My Profile"
Step 6: Fill in with your website URL and name, then click on "Save Changes"
Step 7: You will find your link here
