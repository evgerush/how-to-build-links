How to get a Backlink on weheartit.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Enter your email address, set your birthday and click on "Next"
Step 3: Create a username and password, then click on "Create account"
Step 4: Select at least 4 inspirations
Step 5: Click on "I'm done!"
Step 6: Select "Settings" in the top right corner
Step 7: Enter your website URL and click on "Save changes"
Step 8: You will find your link here
