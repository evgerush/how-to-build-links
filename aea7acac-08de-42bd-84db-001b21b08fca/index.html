How to get a Backlink on fr.vefblog.net
Step 1: The website is in foreign language, we recommend you to use Google Chrome browser and use the translation feature
Step 2: Click on "Create a blog"
Step 3: Fill in the required fields
Step 4: Choose a template and proceed
Step 5: Log in with your credentials
Step 6: Click on "Write your first article"
Step 7: Fill in with title, short description and your website URL, then click on "Post" (use link button to enter link)
Step 8: You will find your link here
