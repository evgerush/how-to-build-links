How to get a Backlink on wow.link
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields, then click on "Sign Up"
Step 3: Enter verification code from your mailbox, then click on "Please confirm the action"
Step 4: Click on "Go to main page"
Step 5: Select "Create"
Step 6: Enter your website URL, then click on "Create"
Step 7: You will find your link here
