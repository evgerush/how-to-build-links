How to get a Backlink on instructables.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign Me Up!"
Step 3: Verify your account by clicking link sent to your mailbox
Step 4: Click on "Profile"
Step 5: Select "Edit Profile"
Step 6: Fill in with your website URL and name, then click on "Save Profile"
Step 7: You will find your link here
