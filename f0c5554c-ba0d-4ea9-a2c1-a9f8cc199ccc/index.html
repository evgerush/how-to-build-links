How to get a Backlink on fc2.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on the designated button
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on the provided link
Step 4: Fill in the required fields and click on "Register"
Step 5: Click on "Add service"
Step 6: Find "FC2 Blog" and click on "Add this service"
Step 7: Fill in the required fields, name your blog and click on the designated button
Step 8: Click on "Post Entry"
Step 9: Fill in with title, short description and your website URL, then click on "Save this entry" (Use "link" button to add link)
Step 10: You will find your link here
