How to get a Backlink on websitebuilder.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Select "I want a blog", choose a category, then click on "Continue"
Step 3: Create a name for your site, then click on "Continue"
Step 4: Skip all the steps by clicking on "Continue" until you will get to the last one
Step 5: Enter your email address and click on "Finish"
Step 6: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign up!"
Step 7: Fill in with short description and your website URL, then click on "Publish"
Step 8: You will find your link here
