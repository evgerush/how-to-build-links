How to get a Backlink on mifare.net
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Register"
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 4: Click on "Login"
Step 5: Log in with your credentials
Step 6: Select "My Profile" in the top right corner
Step 7: Click on "Edit"
Step 8: Enter your website URL and click on "Update Profile"
Step 9: You will find your link here
