How to get a Backlink on peoplebookmarks.com
Step 1: Click on "Login / Register"
Step 2: Choose to "Register"
Step 3: Fill in the required fields
Step 4: Click on "Register"
Step 5: Click on "Submit Bookmark"
Step 6: Fill in the required fields (don't forget to enter your website URL)
Step 7: Agree to the terms and click on "Preview & Submit"
Step 8: You will find your link here
