How to get a Backlink on weezevent.com
Step 1: Enter your email address and click on "Create a free account"
Step 2: Fill in the required fields, then click on "Create an account"
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 4: Click on "I Create My First Event"
Step 5: Fill in with title and set starting date, slecet "Free" and click on "Save"
Step 6: Click on "I Go On With The Configuration"
Step 7: Click on "Configure"
Step 8: Enter your website URL in the "Website" box and fill in the "Description" box with shord description and your website URL, then click on "Save"
Step 9: Click on "Post this event"
Step 10: Enter your phone number and click on "Continue"
Step 11: You will find your link here
