How to get a Backlink on daniweb.com
Step 1: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign Up"
Step 2: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 3: Select "Control Panel" in the top right corner
Step 4: Fill in with title, short description and your website URL, then click on "Update Profile"
Step 5: You will find your link here
