How to get a Backlink on pages10.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Register"
Step 3: Proceed to login screen
Step 4: Sign in with your credentials
Step 5: Click on "New Post"
Step 6: Fill in with title, short description and your website URL, then click on "Publish" (Use "link" button to add link)
Step 7: You will find your link here
