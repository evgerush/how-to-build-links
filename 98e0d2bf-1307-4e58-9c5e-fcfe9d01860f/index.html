How to get a Backlink on sbnation.com
Step 1: Click "Sign Up" in the top right corner
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign Up"
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 4: Select "Edit Profile" in the top right corner
Step 5: Fill in with your website name and URL
Step 6: Click on "Update"
Step 7: You will find your link here
