How to get a Backlink on kraftloft.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Create New Account"
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 4: Click on "Log In"
Step 5: Create a password and click on "Save"
Step 6: Select "Add" in the top right corner
Step 7: Fill in with title, description and choose a category
Step 8: Enter your website name and URL, then click on "Save"
Step 9: You will find your link here
