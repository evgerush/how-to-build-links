How to get a Backlink on community.endnote.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Register"
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 4: After confirmation, click on "Return to EndNote Community"
Step 5: Click on "My Settings"
Step 6: Enable visibility of your private information in the "Privacy" tab under "Preferences" section
Step 7: Click on "Save"
Step 8: Enter your website URL into "Personal Information" tab under "Personal" section
Step 9: Click on "Save"
Step 10: You will find your link here
