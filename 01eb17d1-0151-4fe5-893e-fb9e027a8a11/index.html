How to get a Backlink on www.easel.ly
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Create an Account"
Step 3: Click on "New Design"
Step 4: Click on "Create"
Step 5: Choose to "Add a Body Text"
Step 6: Fill in with short description and your website URL, then click on "Share" (Use "link" button to add link)
Step 7: Choose "Shareable Link"
Step 8: You will find your link here
