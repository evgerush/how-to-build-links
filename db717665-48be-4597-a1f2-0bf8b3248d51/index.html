How to get a Backlink on bit.ai
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Enter your email address and click on "Proceed"
Step 3: You will find your activation code in your mailbox, enter it
Step 4: Fill in the required fields and click on "Proceed"
Step 5: Fill in the required fields and click on "Create Account"
Step 6: Click on "Workspaces"
Step 7: Select "Create Workspace"
Step 8: Name your workspace and click on "Save"
Step 9: Click on "Create New"
Step 10: Choose "Blank Document"
Step 11: Fill in with short description and your website URL, then click on "Share"
Step 12: Open the provided link in your browser
Step 13: You will find your link here
