How to get a Backlink on forum.statcounter.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Register"
Step 3: Click on "Log in"
Step 4: Log in with your credentials
Step 5: Select "Account details" in the top right corner
Step 6: Enter your website URL and click on "Save"
Step 7: You will find your link here
