How to get a Backlink on startupmatcher.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Done, create account!"
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 4: Sign in with your credentials
Step 5: Click on "link icon" in the "About Me" section
Step 6: Enter your website name and URL, then click on "Insert Link"
Step 7: Click on "Save"
Step 8: You will find your link here
