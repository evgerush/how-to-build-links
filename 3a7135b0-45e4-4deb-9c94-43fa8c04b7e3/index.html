How to get a Backlink on zippyshare.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Create my account"
Step 3: Login with your credentials
Step 4: Select "My Account" in the top right corner
Step 5: Select "Account Settings"
Step 6: Enter your website URL (without http:// or https://), then click on "Save Settings"
Step 7: You will find your link here
