How to get a Backlink on wickedfire.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Enter your birthday, then click on "Proceed"
Step 3: Accept the agreement and click on "Register"
Step 4: Fill in the required fields and click on "Complete Registration"
Step 5: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 6: Click on "Profile"
Step 7: Enter your website URL and click on "Save Changes"
Step 8: You will find your link here
