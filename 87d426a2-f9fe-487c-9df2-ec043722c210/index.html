How to get a Backlink on edocr.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Enter your email address and click on "Create Account"
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 4: Click on "Create a Password"
Step 5: Create a password and click on "Save"
Step 6: Click on "Set up Your Profile"
Step 7: Fill in "Bio" and "Website" sections with your website URL and click on "Save"
Step 8: You will find your links here
