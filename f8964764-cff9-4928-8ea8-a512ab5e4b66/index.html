How to get a Backlink on squarespacescheduling.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Click on "Continue with Email"
Step 3: Fill in the required fields and click on "Continue"
Step 4: Fill in the required fields and click on "Next"
Step 5: Select any weekday and click on "Next"
Step 6: Click on "Next"
Step 7: Fill in with appointment name, price and duration, then click on "Next"
Step 8: Click on "Get Started"
Step 9: Click on "Customize your Scheduling Page"
Step 10: Fill in with short description and your website URL, then click on "Save Changes" (Use "link" button to add link)
Step 11: You will find your link here
