How to get a Backlink on behance.net
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Create account"
Step 3: Pick at least 1 topic you like and click on "See my custom feed"
Step 4: Click on "Behance Profile"
Step 5: Click on "Edit Your Profile"
Step 6: Enter your website URL - it will autosave
Step 7: You will find your link here
