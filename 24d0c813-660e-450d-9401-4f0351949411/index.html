How to get a Backlink on instagram.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Next"
Step 3: Set your Birthday and click on "Next"
Step 4: Fill in with verification code from your mailbox
Step 5: Select "Profile" in the top right corner
Step 6: Click on "Edit Profile"
Step 7: Enter your website URL and click on "Submit"
Step 8: You will find your link here
