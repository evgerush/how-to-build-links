How to get a Backlink on godaddy.com
Step 1: Click on "Start for Free"
Step 2: Choose to "Continue with Email"
Step 3: Fill in the required fields, then click on "Create Account"
Step 4: Choose a category and click on "Next"
Step 5: Name your site and click on "Next"
Step 6: Click on "Continue for Free"
Step 7: Fill in with a short description, then edit the button with your website data (name and URL), then click on "Done"
Step 8: Click on "Publish"
Step 9: You will find your link here
