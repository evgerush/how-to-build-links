How to get a Backlink on smashwords.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign me up!"
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 4: Select "Profile" in the top right corner
Step 5: Click on "Complete your profile - Enter your websites"
Step 6: Enter your website URL and click on "Update Profile"
Step 7: You will find your link here
