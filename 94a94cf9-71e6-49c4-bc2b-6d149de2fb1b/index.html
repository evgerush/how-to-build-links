How to get a Backlink on vk.com
Step 1: Fill in the required fields and click on "Continue registration"
Step 2: Enter your phone numer, then click on "Next"
Step 3: You will recieve a verification call, enter last four digits of the calling number and click on "Next"
Step 4: Create a password and click on "Join VK"
Step 5: Click on "Skip"
Step 6: Select "My Profile" in the left side manu
Step 7: Click on "Edit"
Step 8: Choose "Contact info"
Step 9: Enter your website URL and click on "Save"
Step 10: You will find your link here
