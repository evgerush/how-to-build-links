How to get a Backlink on slideshare.net
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Choose to sign up with email
Step 3: Fill in the required fields and click on "Sign Up"
Step 4: Click on "Upload"
Step 5: Click on "Select files to upload" and upload your PDF file that contains your website URL
Step 6: Fill in with title, short description, enter tags, choose a category, set privacy to "Public", then click on "Publish"
Step 7: You will find your link here
