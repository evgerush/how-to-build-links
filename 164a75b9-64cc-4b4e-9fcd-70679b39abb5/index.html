How to get a Backlink on traffup.net
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Fill in the required fields and click on "Create Account"
Step 3: Go to your mailbox and click on the activation link
Step 4: Click on "Websites"
Step 5: Click on "Add Website"
Step 6: Enter your website URL and click on "Continue"
Step 7: Fill in with title, short description and select a category, then click on "Add Website"
Step 8: You will find your link here
