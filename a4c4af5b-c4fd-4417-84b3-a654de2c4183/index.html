How to get a Backlink on disqus.com
Step 1: Open an account
Step 2: Create your account
Step 3: Choose "I want to comment on sites" option
Step 4: Click on your profile picture
Step 5: Click on "Edit Profile"
Step 6: Fill in with your website URL and click on "Save"
Step 7: You will find your link here
